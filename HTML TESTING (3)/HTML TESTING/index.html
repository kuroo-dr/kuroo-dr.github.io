ABOUT

Welcome to Comlit- a website for learning more about computers, both informationally and educationally,that is, for students who want more in-depth understanding of computers. You'll fing the basics of computer hardware, software appications, and some insights into programming.

The goal of ComLit is to foster digital literacy by providing accesible, interactive, and approachable computer science education. We aim to help students and approachable computer skills for modern learning and everyday life.

We aspire to a community of learners who are competent and confident with technology and who are able to adapt tp technology's growing footprint in our world.

ComLit was developed by a collaborative group of enthusiastic students and educators who care about digital literacy. We aim to create this resource to help make computer literacy simple, enjoyable and effective for everyone
